Fees & what you received

16 February 2026 opening invoice: R15,000 once-off, split as in the table. The R4,000 line was the setup month (Facebook and ad account work). After that invoice, ongoing service is R3,000/month (not R4,000 every month).

Opening invoice: what you paid for

Line item Amount What you got
Website (once-off) R5,000 miwesufirewood.co.za. Full Next.js site (70+ pages): products (Sekelbos, Geelhaak, Braai Mix), wood pages, 47 Gauteng delivery-area SEO pages, policies, WhatsApp ordering, Google structured data, internal tools. Discounted vs a typical custom build (e.g. your photography input).
Facebook & ads: setup & first-month service R4,000 One-time on the opening invoice: Facebook page and ad setup, campaigns, creative direction, research and strategy (the heavy work before a steady monthly rhythm). This is not ad spend. It is labour.
Meta ad spend (your budget) R6,000 Budget for ads on Meta, paid through to Facebook (not kept as agency fee). Proof: PDFs under Billing. Total on this report (inc VAT): n/a.

R5,000 + R4,000 + R6,000 = R15,000.

Agency work vs money to Meta

  • Work to you (agency): R9,000 (website R5,000 + opening Facebook/ad setup month R4,000).
  • To Meta for ads: the R6,000 budget. Exact charged total from receipts is above and under Billing & PDF proof.

From month 2 onward

Ongoing service fee R3,000/month (after the R4,000 setup line on the opening invoice). Ad spend is separate. Example: another R6,000 to Meta in one month could mean R9,000 out of pocket that month (R3,000 service + R6,000 ads). Only R3,000 is the agency fee; the rest goes to Facebook.

Ads not running? When Meta budget runs out, delivery stops until the account is topped up. The R3,000 fee is creative and management. It does not add new ad budget by itself. Compare the Billing tab to what you agreed to load.

What the ongoing retainer includes

  • Creative, formats, iterations, scheduling (including April 2026 Mon / Wed / Fri posts on this site).
  • Research and strategy (documented); monitoring and tweaks while ads run.
  • This portal: receipts, performance export, April downloads so you can check numbers yourself.
Why the opening split looks like this

Versus a smaller monthly bundle (more service, less ad line), this deal put a R6,000 ad slice on the opening total, plus a discounted website and a R4,000 setup month before the R3,000 ongoing fee.
